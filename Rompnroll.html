Romp n' Roll

Website: https://rompnroll.com/richmond-va/

Romp n' Roll is similar to Sunshine Play, in set-up, activities, and access structure.

Pros: Never gets crowded due to the reservation system, and very toddler safe.

Cons: Not great to last-minute plans as you have to sign up for classes in advance.

Rating
  • Convenience/Accessibility: 3/5 Requires signups for predesignated time slots, with which you'll be in a group/class.
  • Toddler-Proofing: 5/5 Modestly-sized, well-padded, and clean.
  • Repeatability: 3.5/5 It's smaller and more structured than some comparible places, so it may get old after a few visits.
