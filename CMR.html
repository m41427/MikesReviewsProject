The Children's Museum of Richmond

Website: https://www.childrensmuseumofrichmond.org/

The Children's Museum is a beloved local institution located right next to the Science Museum of Virginia. With both indoor and outside play areas, creative activities, a functional merry-go-round, and countless fun stations for them to explore, it's a sure-fire hit with kids of all ages.

Pros: Cheap, fun, all-weather availability, and conveniently located in the heart of Richmond.

Cons: None.
